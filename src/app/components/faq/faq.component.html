Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping, shipping, and our products

  • How do I place an order?

    To place an order, simply browse our products, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You’ll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, Paystack, and direct bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.

  • How can I track my order?

    Once your order is shipped, we’ll send you a confirmation email with tracking details. You can also log in to your account to view the status of your order at any time.

  • What is your return policy?

    We offer a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return or exchange.

  • Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we ship to several countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location. Please refer to our shipping policy for more details.

  • Can I change or cancel my order?

    You can modify or cancel your order before it is shipped. Once your order has been dispatched, changes are no longer possible. Please contact our support team as soon as possible for any adjustments.
